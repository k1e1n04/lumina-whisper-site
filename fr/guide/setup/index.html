Initial Setup

Menu Bar Icon

After launching the app, the LuminaWhisper icon appears in the menu bar. Click it to open Settings.

Checking the Hotkey

The default hotkey is Fn.

Model Download

On first launch, the small model downloads automatically. You can switch to tiny, base, or medium in the General tab of Settings. Voice input is available even while a model is downloading.
